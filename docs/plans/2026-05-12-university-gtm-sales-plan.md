# University GTM Sales Plan: Admissions-Season AI Messaging

*Date: 2026-05-12*
*Owner: GTM / Sales*
*Context: Rector-level university opportunity; compare public university AI assistants and position Qualy for admissions-period WhatsApp + Instagram demand.*

## Executive Read

The public examples shared by the rector's circle mostly solve **institutional knowledge access**: a web assistant or internal portal that answers questions from official university sources. They are useful and politically attractive because they can be framed as "our IT team built this."

Qualy's best sales angle should not be "we also build a university chatbot." That turns the conversation into a build-vs-buy fight with IT. The stronger angle is:

> Qualy is an admissions-period AI messaging operations layer: it connects Instagram DM and WhatsApp, answers from approved university content, captures candidate intent, summarizes every conversation, and hands serious or risky conversations to humans in one inbox.

For universities, the urgent pain is not only "can students ask a question on a website?" It is:

- prospective students ask repetitive questions through Instagram and WhatsApp during high-volume preference periods;
- social media teams and student affairs cannot consistently answer fast;
- the university cannot easily see which programs, scholarships, dorms, fees, or international topics drive demand;
- risky or official/edge questions need a human handoff;
- leadership wants a modern AI story without waiting for a full internal build.

- Live assistant: https://webrobot.selcuk.edu.tr/
- Update notes: https://webrobot.selcuk.edu.tr/guncellemeler

What it is:

- Announced on 2026-05-04 by Selcuk University IT Department.
- Built by the university's IT department as a "Yapay Zeka Destekli Bilgi Islem Asistani / Web Robot."
- Main scope in the announcement: IT service and workflow information such as wireless/VPN settings, EBYS support, academic/admin contact info, corporate email/password processes, and other IT processes.
- Access model: IT department web page chat area or direct webrobot URL.
- Public assistant UI shows versioning, feedback, source links, a warning that critical information should be verified, and a sidebar/history-style chat interface.
- Update notes say v1.3 was current on 2026-04-30 and describe technical support guidance; prior notes mention corporate staff/contact directory, service/application guides, semantic search, PDF/document analysis beta, and a user suggestion portal.

What it is not, based on public pages:

- Not presented as an admissions conversion or campaign inbox product.
- Not presented as Instagram DM or WhatsApp integration.
- Not presented as lead/intake scoring, operator takeover workflow, or social conversation CRM.
- Not sold as a repeatable SaaS package for multiple institutions.

Sales implication:

Selcuk proves that rectors now find AI assistant projects legitimate. But it also gives IT a build-it-yourself reference. We should use Selcuk as a category validator, then pivot: "That solves web/IT knowledge access. Our proposal solves admissions-season social messaging and candidate follow-up."

## Competitive Pattern

The examples cluster into three patterns:

| Pattern | What the university gets | Where it is strong | Where Qualy can differentiate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Web knowledge assistant | Public or internal chat over official pages/docs | Fast institutional information access, easy rector narrative | Usually no social inbox, no candidate conversation pipeline, weak operator workflow |
| Internal authenticated AI workspace | AI for students/personnel with history, upload, Telegram, campus data | Strong internal IT/control story | Often not optimized for public admissions, WhatsApp/Instagram, handoff, and conversion visibility |
| NotebookLM/source notebook | Very fast launch over curated sources | Low-cost, transparent source scope | No productized messaging ops, no CRM, no multi-channel routing, no live team workflow |

## "Why Buy Instead Of Building Internally?"

This objection is central. The answer should respect IT instead of fighting it.

### Executive answer

> Bilgi islem elbette kurum icinde bilgi asistani gelistirebilir. Bizim onerimiz onu ikame etmek degil; tercih donemi gibi zaman-kritik bir donemde Instagram ve WhatsApp mesaj operasyonunu hazir, guvenli ve raporlanabilir sekilde devreye almak. Icerik ve onay universitede kalir; kanal entegrasyonu, gelen kutusu, AI guardrail, insan devri ve raporlama katmanini Qualy saglar.

### Practical build-vs-buy points

- Speed: admissions season has a calendar. A pilot can be live in days/weeks instead of an internal backlog cycle.
- Channel complexity: Instagram/WhatsApp messaging, Meta app review, webhook verification, 24-hour messaging windows, media handling, and delivery failures are product work, not only prompt work.
- Operational UI: admissions staff need inbox, assignment, summaries, tags/notes, handoff, and unread/state handling.
- Risk control: grounded answers, official source disclaimers, fail-closed behavior, and human takeover are already core Qualy work.
- Measurement: a rector will want proof after the pilot: volume, top topics, unanswered themes, handoff reasons, program interest, response speed.
- IT partnership: university IT can own data source approval, domain/branding, SSO/security review, and final authority; Qualy owns time-to-value and messaging operations.

## University Use Cases

### Admissions / Preference Period

Primary wedge. Questions include:

- program availability and quotas;
- tuition/scholarship/payment information where applicable;
- dorm/accommodation;
- campus/location/transport;
- language of instruction;
- transfer/DGS/YKS preference rules;
- international admissions;
- registration documents and deadlines;
- social life and facilities.

Qualy output:

- answer from approved source;
- ask one useful follow-up if needed;
- summarize candidate profile;
- mark high-intent conversations for admissions team;
- hand off official/edge cases.

### Social Media DM Triage

Instagram posts and reels create bursts of repetitive questions. Qualy can answer first, then route serious questions to staff.

### WhatsApp Admissions Hotline

University can publish one WhatsApp number during campaign period. Qualy handles first response and intake, with humans taking over complex cases.

### Department-Level Mini Pilots

If rector/IT hesitates on all-university rollout, start with one high-demand unit:

- International Office;
- Continuing Education Center;
- Graduate Institute;
- Faculty with strong recruitment pressure;
- A specific vocational school.

## Demo Strategy

The demo should not be a generic product tour. It should simulate a live admissions day.

Demo flow:

1. Candidate asks from Instagram: "Psikoloji bolumu var mi, burs var mi?"
2. Qualy answers from approved admissions content and asks one helpful follow-up.
3. Inbox shows channel, candidate, AI summary, extracted fields, and priority.
4. Candidate asks official/edge question: "Kesin yerlesir miyim?"
5. Qualy gives safe guidance and routes to human instead of overpromising.
6. Operator takes over; AI stops.
7. Dashboard/report shows top questions and missing content.

Demo data to prepare:

- 1 fictional university admissions KB;
- 3 departments/programs;
- scholarship/dorm/transport pages;
- "do not answer as official placement guarantee" rule;
- 10 realistic Turkish candidate messages;
- 2 edge/risk cases;
- 1 international-student case.

## Objection Handling

### "Bilgi islem yapsin."

Agree first:

> Kesinlikle, kurum bilgisinin sahibi bilgi islem ve ilgili birimler olmali.

Then reframe:

> Ama burada satinalinan sey sadece LLM soru-cevap degil. Meta kanallari, mesaj teslimati, tek inbox, insan devri, kaynak siniri, raporlama ve kampanya donemi operasyon hazirligi.

Close:

> Pilot modelinde bilgi islem icerigi ve kurallari onaylar; biz bu onayli kapsamla tercih donemini canliya aliriz. Pilot sonunda istenirse kurum ici ekibe teknik devir/hybrid model de konusulur.

### "Zaten Selcuk/DEU yapmis."

Response:

> Evet, bu iyi haber: kategori dogrulaniyor. Ancak onlarin kamuya acik ornekleri daha cok web/kurumsal bilgi asistani. Biz aday ogrencinin mesaj attigi sosyal kanallardaki operasyonu, takip onceligini ve insan devrini cozuyoruz.

### "AI yanlis bilgi verirse?"

Response:

> Pilot kapsaminda AI yalnizca onayli kaynaklardan yanit verir, kritik ve baglayici konularda resmi kaynak/insan devri yapar, operator istedigi an devralir. Ayrica hangi sorularin eksik kaynak yuzunden cevaplanamadigini raporlar.

### "KVKK / veri guvenligi?"

Response:

> Pilot veri minimizasyonu ile tasarlanir: adaydan gereksiz ozel veri istenmez, sadece iletisim/talep takibi icin gerekli alanlar toplanir. Kurumsal sozlesme, saklama suresi, erisim rolleri, loglama ve silme proseduru baslatmadan netlestirilir.

### "Butce yok."

Response:

> Tam rollout yerine tercih donemi pilotu oneriyoruz. Basari metrikleri net: kac mesaj cevaplandi, kac ciddi aday yakalandi, hangi sorular tekrarlandi, ekip ne kadar operasyon yukunden kurtuldu. Sonraki yil icin butce karari veriyle alinir.

## Product Gap / Readiness Notes

Already strong for this motion:

- multi-channel positioning across WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Messenger;
- unified inbox and operator workflow;
- Knowledge Base grounded responses;
- Skills/guardrails and human handoff;
- AI summaries, required-info collection, lead/status scoring;
- calendar/booking layer, if campus tour or appointment flow is needed;
- sales-led billing/admin activation flow for non-self-serve accounts.

Must be packaged or verified before serious university pitch:

- a university-specific demo KB and demo conversations;
- channel setup path for a university-owned Instagram account and WhatsApp number;
- public-sector/privacy security FAQ;
- pilot reporting template;
- official-source disclaimer template;
- escalation routing map by department;
- an admissions-specific "do not answer" policy;
- whether Messenger is pitch-only/roadmap or live enough for this institution's needs.

Possible product gaps if the university asks for a broader assistant:

- website widget embedding;
- document upload by university staff as a self-serve source pipeline;
- SSO/LDAP/SAML;
- role-based department queues;
- multilingual admissions support beyond TR/EN;
- exportable rector report / PDF;
- integration with CRM/student information systems.

Recommendation:

Keep the first deal scoped to WhatsApp + Instagram admissions messaging. Do not volunteer SSO, full website widget, internal staff assistant, or student information system integration in the first pilot unless they become deal blockers.

## Go / No-Go Criteria

Go if:

- they care about admissions/preference-period message volume;
- Instagram or WhatsApp is an important candidate channel;
- IT is open to partner-led pilot with internal content control;
- a decision-maker can approve a small pilot before campaign season.

No-go or de-scope if:

- they only want an internal general AI assistant;
- they require deep SIS/CRM/SSO integration before any pilot;
- they will not allow WhatsApp/Instagram connection;
- they expect AI to give binding admissions decisions.
